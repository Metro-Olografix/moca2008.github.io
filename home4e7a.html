Where will MOCA 2008 be held MOCA will be held at the Parco della ex caserma Di Cocco ("Park of the former Di Cocco barracks") park.

The park is in the Pretuzi street, very close to the University and the fire station. These landmarks can be used for getting directions.

Download the KMZ placemark for the park.

View themap of the park.

Getting there:

Pescara Centrale train station:
You can reach the MOCA from the Centrale station with Gestione Trasporti Metropolitani (GTM) bus line 1. You can catch it at the stop in corso Vittorio Emanuele II, "Francavilla" route.

Pescara Porta Nuova train station:
You can reach the MOCA from the Porta Nuova station with GTM bus line 1. You can catch it at the stop in viale Gabriele D'Annunzio, "Francavilla" route.

For other routes and timetables visit the site ofGestione Trasporti Metropolitani (in italian).
